What is FormFactor Racing?

FormFactor Racing is a Saturday metro selection service focused on clarity and confidence. No hype, no glossy tipsheets – just one pick per race, a confidence rating, and a short, straight explanation of why that horse is on top.

  • Brand built around data, not personality.
  • Same framework every week so you know what you’re getting.
  • Designed for punters who want help making decisions, not someone to blame.

FAQ

When are the packs updated?

Packs are built for the main Saturday metro meetings. If a meeting shifts or is abandoned, that’ll be reflected in the pack for that week.

What exactly do I see for each race?

One FormFactor selection, a confidence rating, and a short explanation. That’s it. No giant spreadsheets, no walls of text – just the parts you’ll actually use.

Do you share staking plans or tell me how much to bet?

No. FormFactor is a selection and structure service. You stay in control of staking, risk, and bank management. The packs help you decide what to back – not how to manage your money.

Can I try it before going monthly?

Yes – you can start with the Best Bets Pack for $5 or the Full Raw Data Pack for $10. If you like how it fits into your routine, you can swap to the monthly membership later.
